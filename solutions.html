Technical approaches

Cooling systems can be redesigned to reduce water dependence.

Closed-loop systems

  • Recirculate coolant instead of depending on continuous evaporation.
  • Reduce ongoing community freshwater demand.
  • Offer a stronger long-term sustainability baseline.

Direct-to-chip and immersion cooling

  • Bring liquid cooling closer to the heat source.
  • Improve heat transfer for dense AI hardware.
  • Can reduce overall cooling energy when deployed well.

Operational approaches

Infrastructure decisions outside the server rack matter too.

01

Use reclaimed water

Cooling systems do not always need potable water. Recycled and reclaimed water can reduce pressure on drinking water supplies.

02

Choose sites carefully

Cooler climates and less stressed watersheds can reduce the burden of water-intensive thermal management.

03

Reuse waste heat

Heat from data centers can support district heating and other community systems instead of simply being discarded.
